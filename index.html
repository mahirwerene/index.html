Integrations & APIs

To extend your bakery website we recommend adding the following integrations:

  • Google Maps (Embed) — show your shop and calculate delivery distances.
  • Email sending (SendGrid, Mailgun, or SMTP) — server-side reliable email confirmations.
  • EmailJS (client-side) — quick way to send emails from the browser (requires API keys).
  • Payment (Stripe) — accept card payments securely.
  • Social APIs (Instagram Graph, Facebook SDK) — pull reviews or show recent posts.
  • Analytics (Google Analytics / GA4) — track visitors and conversions.

(You can add these by signing up on the provider sites and adding the small snippets / server endpoints; if you'd like, I can generate those snippets tailored to your account.)
